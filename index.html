Borui Gong

Postdoc at PolyU

Welcome! I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I completed my Ph.D. in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Man Ho Au.

My research focuses on zero-knowledge proofs and blockchain technology. I'm particularly passionate about building cryptographic systems that are both theoretically sound and practically efficient. Before diving into cryptography, I studied Software Engineering and Finance (double degree) at Shandong University.

If you're interested in my research or have ideas you'd like to discuss, feel free to reach out! I'm always happy to chat about anything crypto-related.
